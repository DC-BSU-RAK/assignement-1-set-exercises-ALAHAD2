Welcome

Digi Fest is a celebration of digital and technology in the city of Cardiff. This year, we are celebrating 'collaboration', so come and find out about the latest collaborations that are happening in this historic and innovative city. They promise an educational and inspirational packed programme for all.We are thrilled to gather here with innovators, engineers, thought leaders, and industry pioneers who share a common vision: to build open, interoperable, and future-ready communication infrastructures. Over the next few days, we'll engage in collaborative testing, dynamic discussions, and cutting-edge demonstrations that highlight the progress and potential of the DIFI standard. Thank you to all our members, partners, and participants for your dedication, contributions, and passion. Let’s make this another impactful and inspiring DIFI Fest!
