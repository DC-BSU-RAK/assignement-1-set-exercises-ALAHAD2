Chicken Mandi Receipe

Middle Eastern declicious food and Chicken dish

Ingredients

The Hawaj Spice Mix

  • 2 tbsp of coriander seeds/powder
  • 2 tbsp of black pepper corns
  • 2 tbsp of cumin seeds
  • 1 large of cinnamon stick
  • ½ tsp cloves
  • 4 cardamoms
  • 1 tsp of fennel seeds
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp of turmeric powder

    • The Chicken Marinade

  • 6 chicken leg quarters (bone-in, skinless)
  • 3 tbsp of Hawaj spice mix
  • 2 tbsp of ginger garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp of oil
  • Salt (as required)

    • The Rice

  • 3 cups basmati rice (washed and drained)
  • 5¼ cups water
  • 2 bay leaves
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 large onion (thinly sliced)
  • 1 large carrot (sliced into sticks)
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • ½ cup golden raisins
  • 2 dried black lemons
  • 1 magic chicken cube
  • 1 tbsp Hawaj spice mix
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • ¼ cup slivered almonds (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Hawaj Spice Mix: Dry roast the ingredients until aromatic, then grind into a fine powder.
  2. Marinate the Chicken: Mix the marinade ingredients, make gashes on the chicken, and refrigerate for 30 minutes or overnight.
  3. Cook the Rice: Heat oil, add bay leaves, cinnamon stick, and black pepper corns. Sauté until the onions are translucent, then add the rice and water. Cook until the rice is tender.
  4. Cook the Chicken: Pan-fry the marinated chicken until cooked through, then set aside.
  5. Assemble and Serve: Combine the cooked rice and chicken, garnish with slivered almonds, and serve with Salata Hara (Arabic Tomato Salsa).

    6. Salata Hara (Arabic Tomato Salsa)

  6. 3 tomatoes (roughly chopped)
  7. A handful of cilantro leaves
  8. 2 green chilies
  9. 2 garlic cloves
  10. ½ small lemon (squeezed)
  11. Salt (as required)
